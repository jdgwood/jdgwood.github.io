States of Innovation

Driving the American Economy in the 21st Century — Bristol University Press, 2025.

Bristol University Press · 2025

This book unveils the forces behind the dynamic knowledge economy that drove America's 21st century economic success. Challenging the notion of the US as a singular market-driven economy, it introduces the concept of the Polycentric Innovation State to explain the critical role federal and state governments play in shaping innovation-led growth.

Through detailed comparative analysis of four states, the book demonstrates how regional authorities pursue distinct yet overlapping strategies to foster high-value industries. Essential reading for scholars of comparative political economy and American governance, it offers fresh insights into the evolving landscape of American capitalism and state–market relations.
